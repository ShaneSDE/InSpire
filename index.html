Brian Yang Wins First Place in Windsor and Ranks 6th Nationally in the 2024 Kangaroo Math Contest

Brian Yang, a distinguished InSpirer, has achieved outstanding success in the 2024 Kangaroo Math Competition. Demonstrating his extraordinary mathematical talent, Brian has secured first place in Windsor and ranked 6th in Canada.

The majority of Grade 6 students in Ontario failed the province’s standardized math test

The latest data from the Education Quality and Accountability Office (EQAO) reveals that only 59% of Grade 3 students have met the provincial mathematics standards. This figure drops to 47% for Grade 6 students, while just over half, 52%, of Grade 9 students reached the standard. These statistics, confirmed by the Ministry of Education, indicate that math test scores have shown no significant improvement since 2018. In response, the Minister of Education has recommended that students enhance their math skills by participating in after-school tutoring programs.

About Us

Welcome to InSpire Learner Center in Windsor, where each student’s educational journey is customized to their individual needs and interests. We are dedicated to a learner-centered approach, employing innovative tools like games and animations to make learning both enjoyable and engaging.

At InSpire Learner Center, we believe in the power of individualized education. Our curriculum is designed to adapt to each student’s learning style, helping to foster not only a deeper interest in subjects but also the development of critical thinking and problem-solving skills. By focusing on individual strengths and areas for growth, we equip students with the skills they need to address real-world challenges confidently.
